What is the Manor?

Sometimes imaginary friends get lost or kids outgrow them. This is why the Imaginatorium Manor, a home for imaginary friends was started.

When kids dream up pals, they come to life and the house is filled with diverse characters waiting to be reunited or adopted to new kids. We provide a warm, dry, safe place where these friends can reside in the mean while.

Our home is a perfect place for friends and kids alike to focus on building friendships and to learn the importance of friends.
